Answers to common questions

Are the materials provided

All tools and materials are provided, however, you are welcome to bring your own tools - old and new - and learn how to use and maintain them. Please note, it is a hand tool course and no power tools are used.

What to bring to class

All tools and materials are provided, however, you are welcome to bring your own tools Please bring the following PPE: a face mask safety glasses or goggles earmuffs or earplugs We do have PPE sanitised and available to borrow. Participants must sanitise before and after use.

Where is the venue and how do I get there

Unless stated otherwise, all workshops happen in-house at Artizen Studios. We are at 50 Rescue Boulevard, Sydney. The entrance to the studio is via Turner Street, which is at the rear of 100 Goldburn Street. Transportation and parking The studio is located 500m from the Parramatta train station. There is also paid parking on the street nearby.

How do I make payments for the workshops

You can call us and pay using credit card details. Alternatively, you can pay in cash on the day of the workshop.

Anything else I need to know

Please don't wear your best clothing. If you have long hair, please tie it back Wear closed-in shoes
